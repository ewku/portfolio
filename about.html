About me

User experience professional with a keen eye for aesthetics who asks the right questions and seeks answers in qualitative and quantitative data. I plan and execute research, translate the insights into functional solutions to then help build them through an iterative process.

Career path

My career path to user experience research might look unconventional on paper, but that has given me a uniquely broad skillset. For the last year I’ve been freelancing for small businesses to grow my career in the UX field. It has been an especially important part of my development due to the variety of projects I’ve taken on. Now I am eager to continue growing as a UX researcher by applying my skill set in a collaborative, team setting.

My passion is user experience in enterprises and InsurTech/FinTech sectors. I enjoy solving complex problems that challenge my creativity and analytical skills. I am eager to roll up my sleeves and contribute to making impact in complex, often unappreciated, areas. Let's talk UX!
